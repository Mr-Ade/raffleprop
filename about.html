RaffleProp was founded with a simple mission: make property ownership possible for every Nigerian, not just the wealthy. We operate Nigeria's most transparent and legally structured property raffle platform.

Our Story

From Frustration to Solution

RaffleProp was founded in 2024 by Chukwuemeka Adeyemi, a Lagos-based property developer who watched countless Nigerians priced out of the real estate market despite years of hard work and saving.

The average Lagos property now costs ₦45–150 million. For most Nigerians — whether at home or in the diaspora — that's an impossible sum. But ₦2,500 for a shot at owning a home? That's achievable.

We spent 18 months working with Nigerian property lawyers and compliance experts to build a structure that is 100% legal, transparent, and resistant to fraud. The result is RaffleProp.

Legal Framework

Fully Legal & Compliant

CAC Registration
RaffleProp Ltd is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria under RC-1234567. You can verify this at the CAC portal.
Legal Structure
Structured as a Promotional Competition — not a lottery — meaning no federal lottery licence is required. This structure includes a mandatory skill element (answering a question correctly) at point of purchase.
Independent Legal Oversight
Every campaign is overseen by an independent property lawyer. Our current legal partner is Adaeze Obi-Nwosu (Esq.) of Obi-Nwosu & Partners, Lagos. Their contact details are publicly listed.
FCCPC Approval
Every campaign is individually approved by the Federal Competition & Consumer Protection Commission (FCCPC) under the FCCPA 2018 before ticket sales begin. For Lagos properties, we also hold a Lagos State Lotteries & Gaming Authority (LSLGA) licence. Approval letters are public.
SCUML Registration (AML/CFT)
Registered with the Special Control Unit against Money Laundering (SCUML) as a Designated Non-Financial Business and Profession (DNFBP) — Ref: SCUML/DNFBP/2024/RP/8821. We conduct KYC on all participants, file Suspicious Transaction Reports with the NFIU, and retain records for 5 years minimum.
NDPR Compliance
We comply with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR). Your data is encrypted, never sold, and you may request deletion at any time.
