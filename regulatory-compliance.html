Four Layers of Legal Protection

Nigeria's property promotional competition framework requires compliance at the federal level, state level, professional valuation standard, and anti-money laundering regulation simultaneously.

Federal — FCCPA 2018

The Federal Competition & Consumer Protection Act 2018 governs all promotional competitions in Nigeria. Every campaign requires prior FCCPC approval, transparent skill-based entry, and written transaction records for every participant.

FCCPC approval before any ticket sales
Skill question required per §123 (not a lottery)
12-field transaction record per §118 for every buyer
FCCPC notified 14 days before every draw
Form CPC B post-draw report within 21 days
Full refund guarantee if minimum not reached

Lagos State — LSLGA

Lagos-based property promotions additionally require a licence from the Lagos State Lotteries & Gaming Authority. This state-level licence applies on top of federal FCCPC approval and covers operations within Lagos State.

LSLGA Application Fee: ₦50,000
LSLGA Licence Fee: ₦500,000/year
LSLGA Lottery Levy per draw
Good Causes Fund provision — pending legal opinion
Lagos only: Abuja and other state campaigns are covered by FCCPC only. Each campaign detail page shows whether LSLGA applies.

Property Valuation — NIESV

Every property prize must be independently valued by a certified member of the Nigerian Institution of Estate Surveyors & Valuers (NIESV) before the FCCPC application is filed. This prevents inflated or fictitious prize values.

NIESV-certified valuer engaged before application
Valuation certificate submitted to FCCPC (Form CPC A)
Valuer's name and FNIVS/MNIVS credentials published
Download valuation certificate from each campaign page

AML/CFT — SCUML

RaffleProp is registered with the Special Control Unit against Money Laundering (SCUML) as a Designated Non-Financial Business and Profession (DNFBP) under the Money Laundering (Prohibition) Act. This ensures our platform cannot be used for financial crime.

SCUML Reg: SCUML/DNFBP/2024/RP/8821
KYC (Customer Due Diligence) on all buyers
Suspicious Transaction Reports filed with NFIU
5-year minimum transaction record retention
Designated AML/CFT Compliance Officer appointed
Annual AML/CFT risk assessment conducted
DNFBP Status: As a property competition operator collecting public funds, RaffleProp falls under SCUML's designated business category — subjecting us to the same AML standards as estate agents and gaming operators.
