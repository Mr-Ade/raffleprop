FCCPA 2018 §123 — Stated Representations Must Be Honoured

If the minimum threshold of tickets is not reached by the close date, the promotion must not proceed. All ticket purchase monies must be refunded within the timeframe stated in the approved T&Cs. A Form CPC B must be filed with the FCCPC noting that the promotion did not proceed. Failure to refund is an offence under the FCCPA.
