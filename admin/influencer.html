FCCPA 2018 — All Influencer Content Must Carry FCCPC Approval Statement

Every piece of influencer content promoting a RaffleProp campaign must include the FCCPC approval statement. Before any content goes live: (1) admin must upload the approved content brief issued to the influencer, (2) confirm the FCCPC approval statement is included, (3) confirm content has been reviewed and approved. Content cannot be marked "Approved for Use" without completing all three steps.
