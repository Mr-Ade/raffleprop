FCCPA 2018 — Mandatory Post-Draw Obligations

14 days before draw: Notify FCCPC in writing of draw date, venue, and method (FCCPA 2018 §124).
3 days after draw: Submit winner details to FCCPC for verification.
21 days after draw: File Form CPC B post-draw report with FCCPC.
12 months from approval: Sell-in period expires — ticket sales must close and all unsold campaigns returned to FCCPC for re-approval.
