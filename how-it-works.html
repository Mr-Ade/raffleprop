Escrow Protection

How Escrow Protects You

All ticket sale proceeds are held in a dedicated escrow account at a reputable Nigerian bank — separate from RaffleProp's operating funds. This means your money is protected regardless of what happens.

Funds Locked Until Draw

Money cannot be withdrawn until draw completion and winner verification is confirmed.

Auto-Refund If Min Missed

If minimum ticket threshold isn't reached, escrow automatically triggers full refunds within 5–10 business days.

Publicly Disclosed

Escrow bank name and account details are published on every campaign page. You can verify independently.

Refund Policy

What Happens If Minimum Isn't Reached?

Your Money is Always Safe

If ticket sales don't reach the stated minimum threshold before the campaign close date, the campaign is cancelled and every ticket holder receives a 100% full refund. This is guaranteed in our Terms & Conditions and enforced through the escrow arrangement.
