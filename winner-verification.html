Step 2 — Bank Verification Number (BVN)
Your BVN is required to verify your identity against CBN records. This is mandatory for all prize transfers above ₦500,000 under SCUML/AML requirements.
Your BVN is encrypted in transit and at rest. RaffleProp stores only a masked reference (e.g. *****5678). Full BVN is transmitted directly to our KYC partner and never stored in plain text.
Dial *565*0# on any Nigerian network to retrieve your BVN
Step 3 — National Identification Number (NIN)
Your NIN is required by NIMC Act and FCCPA regulations for winner identity verification. It must match the name on your uploaded ID document.
Dial *346# (MTN) or *346# (Airtel/Glo) to retrieve your NIN
Step 4 — Bank Account Details
Provide the Nigerian bank account where any cash component of the prize should be transferred. The account name must match your verified legal name.
Account details will be cross-verified with the CBN Bank Verification System before transfer is initiated. Transfers comply with SCUML AML/CFT requirements.
