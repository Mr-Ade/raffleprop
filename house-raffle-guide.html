Phase 01 — Before Anything Else

Legal Foundation & Compliance

🚨 Do NOT launch before completing this phaseRunning an unlicensed raffle in Nigeria can be classified as illegal lottery or fraud. Payment platforms can freeze your accounts. Participants can sue. You can face criminal liability. Do this first, without exception.
01

Hire a Property Lawyer (Mandatory)

Engage a licensed Nigerian lawyer experienced in property transactions. They will:

  • Verify the house title — Certificate of Occupancy (C of O), Governor's Consent, or Deed of Assignment
  • Confirm no encumbrances (mortgages, disputes, liens) exist on the property
  • Draft a legally binding Raffle Terms and Conditions document
  • Draft a Winner Transfer Agreement to be executed after the draw
  • Advise on correct legal structure (promotional competition vs. lottery)

Estimated cost: ₦200,000 – ₦500,000

02

Structure as a "Competition" Not a Lottery

A pure lottery in Nigeria requires a federal license under the National Lottery Act 2005 — expensive and slow. Instead, structure it as a promotional competition and add a minimal skill element (e.g., "Answer: what colour is Nigeria's flag?"). This sidesteps lottery classification in most states. Your lawyer will advise the exact approach for your state.

03

Register a Business Entity with the CAC

Register with the Corporate Affairs Commission to receive payments legitimately:

  • Business Name Registration — fast (1–3 days), affordable (~₦10,000 at cacregistration.gov.ng)
  • Limited Liability Company — more credible for large transactions (1–2 weeks, ~₦30,000–₦100,000)

Then open a dedicated corporate bank account for raffle proceeds. Never mix with personal funds.

04

Draft and Publish Full Terms & Conditions

Publish before selling any ticket. Must clearly state:

  • Full property description — address, size, title type, current valuation
  • Ticket price and all purchase methods accepted
  • Total tickets available (hard cap — never exceed this number)
  • Draw date, time, and venue
  • How the winner is selected — specify RNG or physical draw method
  • Refund policy — exactly what triggers a full refund to all buyers
  • What happens if the minimum ticket threshold is not reached
  • Tax obligations of the winner
  • Property transfer timeline after the draw
  • Dispute resolution procedure
  • Your CAC registration number, company name, and full contact details
05

Get an Independent Property Valuation

Hire a certified estate valuer registered with the Nigerian Institution of Estate Surveyors and Valuers (NIESV) to produce a formal valuation report. This proves the property value is genuine — one of your strongest trust and marketing assets.

Estimated cost: ₦50,000 – ₦200,000

06

Set Up an Escrow Arrangement (Strongly Recommended)

Partner with a Nigerian bank or licensed escrow service to hold all ticket proceeds. Funds are released to you only after the draw is confirmed and winner verified. This is the single most powerful trust signal you can offer — promote it in every marketing asset.

Nigerian banks offering escrow: GTBank, Zenith Bank, First Bank, Stanbic IBTC. Also explore fintech escrow solutions.

Phase 02 — The Product

Property Documentation & Presentation

The house is your product. Present it with the quality of a premium real estate listing — because buyers are handing over real money based entirely on what you show them.

📋Documents to Gather

  • Certificate of Occupancy (C of O) or equivalent title
  • Deed of Assignment (if applicable)
  • Current stamped survey plan
  • Independent NIESV valuation report
  • Building plan approval (if applicable)
  • Utility receipts showing active connections
  • Property tax receipts (land use charge or tenement rates)
  • Structural/engineering inspection report if available

📷Visual Assets to Create

  • Professional photography — all rooms, exterior, compound, neighbourhood
  • Drone/aerial footage of property and surroundings
  • 10-minute walkthrough video tour, professionally shot and edited
  • 60–90 second teaser video for social media
  • 3D virtual tour or floor plan diagram
  • Neighbourhood context photos — proximity to roads, markets, amenities
  • Before/after photos if any renovations were done

📄Create a Property Information Pack (PDF)

Compile everything into a polished, downloadable PDF that buyers can read and share. Include property photos, valuation summary, location map, title description, FAQ, T&C summary, and how to buy a ticket. Link to this from every marketing touchpoint — it converts fence-sitters into buyers.

Ticket System — Three Options

1Dedicated Raffle Platform (Easiest)

Phase 07 — Draw Day

Conducting the Draw — Step by Step

⚠ The draw is your most legally and reputationally sensitive momentExecute it with total transparency, independent witnesses, and professional video documentation. Any perception of manipulation will destroy trust and expose you to legal action.
1

Choose Your Draw Method — Use Both

  • Digital Random Number Generator: Use Random.org — publicly verifiable and internationally trusted. Record the entire screen during the draw. The timestamped result is your primary record.
  • Physical Draw: Print all ticket numbers, seal in a drum or box, and have an independent person (notary public, lawyer, or respected community figure) draw a ticket on camera in front of live audience.

Using both methods is the most credible approach. Digital RNG confirms, physical draw validates — hardest to dispute.

2

Draw Day Logistics

  • Secure a venue — the property itself is the most credible option; a hotel event room is the alternative
  • Invite: your property lawyer, a notary public or commissioner for oaths, a bank representative from your escrow institution, and at least 2 independent community witnesses
  • Hire a professional video crew to record the event start to finish
  • Set up simultaneous live streams on Facebook, YouTube, and Instagram — publicise the link 48 hours in advance
  • Hire a professional MC to explain each step of the draw process clearly on camera
  • Display the full list of issued ticket numbers on a screen for public verification
3

After the Draw — Winner Verification

  • Announce the winning ticket number live on camera and all active streams
  • Contact the winner immediately via phone call, WhatsApp, email, and SMS simultaneously
  • Give the winner 48–72 hours to respond and come forward — state this in T&Cs and announce it at the draw
  • Winner must provide: valid government-issued ID, BVN for banking verification, and proof of ticket purchase
  • Your lawyer verifies identity and initiates the property transfer process
  • If winner cannot be reached within the stated period, proceed to the next drawn ticket — this must be written into T&Cs and announced
4

Property Transfer to Winner

  • Your lawyer prepares a Deed of Assignment transferring the property to the winner
  • Both parties sign the deed before a notary public
  • File for Governor's Consent at the relevant State Land Bureau if required in your state
  • Hand over all original title documents, keys, survey plan, and a formal handover letter
  • Document the entire handover on video and publish it as your final campaign post — this becomes powerful evidence and marketing for any future ventures
  • Full title processing typically takes 2–8 weeks after deed signing depending on the state
5

Fund Release and Post-Draw Settlement

  • Upon confirmed draw completion and winner verification, escrow agent releases all held funds to your corporate account
  • Deduct all agreed costs: platform commissions, marketing spend, legal fees, operational costs
  • Pay all referral commissions within 7 business days
  • File appropriate tax returns with FIRS (Federal Inland Revenue Service) on your proceeds
  • Post a public thank-you announcement to all 25,000 ticket buyers — this goodwill is invaluable for future campaigns

In Nigeria's current environment, trust is your actual product — not the house. Nigerians have seen too many online scams to hand over ₦2,500 without being convinced you are legitimate. Every single trust signal in this guide is non-optional.

The escrow, the named lawyer, the live draw, the transparent daily communication — these are not extras. They are the foundation. Without them, your marketing spend will be wasted on skeptics who will not convert regardless of how good the property is.

Launch carefully and credibly. Let your first 500 buyers become vocal public advocates. Respond to every comment. Show your face. Go live at the property. Be human, transparent, and consistent. A raffle that feels legitimate because it is legitimate will sell itself.
