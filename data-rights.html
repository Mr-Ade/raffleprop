Data Rights Centre

Manage your personal data rights under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019.

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019 and the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to access, correct, delete, and transfer your personal data. RaffleProp is obligated to respond to all data rights requests within 30 days. Note: certain data (FCCPA §118 transaction records, T&C acceptance logs) must be retained regardless of deletion requests due to legal obligations — these will be noted in your response.
Download My Data
Right of Access — NDPR Art. 3.1(6)

Export all personal data RaffleProp holds about you — including profile info, ticket purchases, T&C acceptances, referral records, and communication preferences — in a machine-readable format.

Delivered by email within 7 days
Correct My Data
Right to Rectification — NDPR Art. 3.1(7)

Update inaccurate or incomplete personal information held about you. You can update most profile details directly from your Profile page. For KYC records, submit a correction request below.

Edit Profile
Delete My Account
Right to Erasure — NDPR Art. 3.1(9)

Request deletion of your account and associated personal data. Note: FCCPA §118 transaction records and T&C acceptance logs are legally required to be retained and cannot be deleted per FCCPA 2018.

This action is irreversible. Ensure you have downloaded your data first.
Manage Consent
Right to Object — NDPR Art. 3.1(11)

Control how your data is used for marketing, analytics, and communications. Withdraw or update your consent at any time.

Data Portability
Right to Portability — NDPR Art. 3.1(6)

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON or CSV) that can be transferred to another service provider.

Lodge a Complaint
Right to Complain — NDPR Art. 3.1(12)

If you believe RaffleProp has not fulfilled a data rights request or has processed your data unlawfully, you can file a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC).

NDPC — ndpc.gov.ng
