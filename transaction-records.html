Permanent legal records of all your ticket purchases under FCCPA 2018 §118.

My Tickets
These records are permanently stored and cannot be deleted. Under the Federal Competition & Consumer Protection Act 2018 §118, every transaction record is a legal document containing mandatory fields. Records must be retained for 7 years per FCCPA regulatory requirements.
FCCPA §118 mandatory fields included in every record: Company name & registered address · Transaction date & reference · Ticket numbers · Campaign description · Unit price · Quantity · Total price · VAT (7.5%) · Grand total.
