FCCPC Approvals

FCCPC Approval Letters

Every RaffleProp campaign is approved by the Federal Competition & Consumer Protection Commission (FCCPC) under the Federal Competition & Consumer Protection Act 2018 (FCCPA) before any ticket sales begin. Approval letters are issued per campaign.

LSLGA Licences

Lagos State Lotteries & Gaming Authority Licences

Lagos-based campaigns additionally require a licence from the Lagos State Lotteries & Gaming Authority (LSLGA). This licence is renewed annually (fee: ₦575,000 per licence year). Licence documents are issued per campaign.

Terms

Terms & Conditions

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

These Terms & Conditions ("Terms") govern your participation in promotional competitions operated by RaffleProp Ltd (RC-1234567). By purchasing a ticket, you agree to be bound by these Terms.

2. Eligibility

Open to Nigerian residents and diaspora aged 18 and above. Employees of RaffleProp Ltd and their immediate family members are not eligible to participate.

3. Ticket Purchase

All ticket purchases are final. Tickets are non-transferable except as permitted by RaffleProp. Participants must answer the skill question correctly to complete entry.

4. Minimum Threshold

Each campaign specifies a minimum ticket threshold. If this threshold is not met before the campaign close date, the campaign shall be cancelled and all ticket holders shall receive a full refund within 5–10 business days.

5. Draw Process

Draws are conducted via certified Random Number Generator (Random.org) with a physical ballot backup. All draws are live-streamed and witnessed by an independent lawyer, notary public, bank representative, and two community members.

6. Winner Verification

Winners must respond within 48–72 hours and submit valid government-issued ID and BVN. Failure to respond within 7 days results in forfeiture and a second draw.

7. Property Transfer

RaffleProp shall bear all legal costs for preparing the Deed of Assignment and filing for Governor's Consent. The winner shall bear no additional fees related to title transfer.

8. Data Protection

We process personal data in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR). See our Privacy Policy for full details.

9. Governing Law

These Terms are governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Lagos State.

Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025 | Compliant with NDPR

1. Data We Collect

Name, email, phone number, payment references (NOT card details), BVN (winners only), device and browser information for security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

To process ticket purchases, send confirmations, conduct draws, verify winners, send marketing communications (with consent only), and comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Security

All sensitive data (BVN, NIN) is encrypted at rest using AES-256. We never store card numbers or CVV. All communications are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

4. Your Rights (NDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. To request data deletion, go to your Profile settings or email privacy@raffleprop.ng. We will process requests within 30 days.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies for security and analytics cookies (GA4, Meta Pixel) with your consent. You can withdraw consent at any time.
